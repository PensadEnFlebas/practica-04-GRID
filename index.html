ATLANTA HAWKS

Founded in 1946 and after traveling from Missisipi river zone to Milkwaukee and St. Louis, finally the basketball franchise settled since 1968 in Georgia's capital: Atlanta. During St. Louis era the Hawks won its only NBA championship in 1958.
